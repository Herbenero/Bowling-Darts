Bowling Darts

Enter player names, then on each frame throw 1 dart to select an alley, and use darts 2 and 3 to score on that alley.

Rules

Select Alley First dart chooses your alley by landing on a segment.
Triple 9 pins on dart 2, spare on dart 3.
Outer Single 3 pins, spare on dart 3.
Double Strike = 10 pins.
Inner Single 7 pins, spare on dart 3.
Miss Counts as 0.
Add players to begin.

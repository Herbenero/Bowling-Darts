Bowling Darts

First dart selects the alley. Darts 2 and 3 score the selected alley. Miss is always 0.

Rules

Dart 1 Choose alley: Miss, Triple, Outer Single, Double, Inner Single.
Dart 2 Score points on the selected alley.
Dart 3 Complete the frame, or spare/bonus if eligible.
Double Strike = 10 pins.
Triple 9 pins, or 20 if hit twice in the frame.
Outer Single 3 pins, or 10 if hit twice in the frame.
Inner Single 7 pins, or 14 if hit twice in the frame.
Miss Counts as 0.
Add players to begin.

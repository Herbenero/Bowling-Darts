Bowling Darts

First dart selects the alley. Darts 2 and 3 score the selected alley. Double on dart 1 or 2 ends the frame unless it is the 10th frame. Double on dart 3 is not a strike.

Rules

Dart 1 Choose alley: 1-20 or buttons.
Dart 2 Score points on the selected alley.
Dart 3 Complete the frame, or spare/bonus if eligible.
Strike Double on dart 1 or dart 2 ends the frame unless it is the 10th frame.
Dart 3 Double on dart 3 does NOT count as a strike.
Symbols Strike = X, Spare = /, Open = -.
